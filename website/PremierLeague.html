Manchester City is on a Roll

The team at the top of the table is from Manchester and we arent talking about you Manchester United. Manchester City has been taking the premier League by storm this season scoring a whopping 31 goals in only 11 season games, including an impressive 5-0 win vs the hot liverpool team. One of the key sucsesses of this season have come by the form of Kevin De Bruyne. The former Chelsea man is on pace to win BPL player of the year and its only November. Sergio aguero and Gabriel Jesus are leading the team in goals scored. Leroy Sane is a menacing force on the left wing using his pace to destroy defenders. If Manchester City keeps playing like this they should win the league no problem.
