Ronaldo Wins The Best Award

Ronaldo has won The Best award only given to the best soccer player in the world for that year. He beat Messi and Neymar to this award. This is his 2nd time in a row winning this prestigious award and he doesn't look like he will be slowing down anytime soon. The portugese Man won the Champions league, Euros and scored 42 goals in all competitions on his way to winning the award. Although he has worked hard to get this award he has struggled in the beginning ofd the 2017-2018 season with only 2 goals in 11 games.
Fun Fact: He has had more kids(3) since the start of the season thn he has goals.
