Neymar Signs for PSG

Neymar has signed for French Giants, PSG. Neymar was unsettled with the management in Barcelona and felt a change was necesary in order to progress in his career. PSG and Barcelona agreed to a fee of a staggering $222 Million to sign the Brazillian star. PSG also made a signifigant addition to their squad by adding wonderboy Kylian Mbappe. With the likes of Dani Alves, Neymar, Mbappe, Cavani, Di Maria, and Verrati, PSG should be a major contender in European competition.
